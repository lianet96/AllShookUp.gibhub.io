The Story

           David is the new boy in town, arriving in the small 1950s community with a love for music that matches John's. Rose, who has grown up with John and always had a secret crush on him, introduces David to John in the hopes that David can help her win over John's heart.However, as John and David spend more time together, their shared passion for music brings them closer, and they start to realize that their feelings for each other go beyond friendship. As Rose observes the growing connection between John and David, she must confront her own unrequited love and come to terms with the possibility of a love that was never meant to be.
